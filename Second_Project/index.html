5 Wonderful Places On Earth


Zhangye Danxia

Location

China

Best time to visit

June to September

How to reach

Fly directly to Lanzhou or Xining and then transfer to railway. The high-speed railway only takes 4 hours from Lanzhou to Zhangye and 2 hours from Xining to Zhangye.

Red Beach, China

Location

China

Best time to visit

May to October

How to reach

Get to Panjin City by bus or train from anywhere in China.

Giant Crystal Cave, Mexico

Location

Mexico

Best time to visit

December to April

How to reach

Fly to Chihuahua and take a bus or train to Naica. From here you can get a taxi to the caves.

Mendenhall Ice Caves, Alaska

Location

Alaska

Best time to visit

mid May to mid September

How to reach

Ride to Tongass National Forest in Juneau and then trek to the Mendenhall Glacier Ice Caves with a guide.

Under-sea Waterfall, Mauritius

Location

Mauritius

Best time to visit

September to December

How to reach

Basically, there are two options to see Mauritius sand waterfall illusion, or rather, two means of transportation: a helicopter and a seaplane. Both can get quite expensive beyond the reasonable reach of common travelers.
